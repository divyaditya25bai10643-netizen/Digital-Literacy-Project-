# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
 

Do’s
- Use respectful and polite language in all posts and messages.  
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.  
- Keep personal and sensitive information private.  
- Respond professionally to messages, comments, and online discussions.  
- Report inappropriate or harmful content to the platform authorities.
 
Don’ts
- Don’t post offensive, harmful, or discriminatory content.  
- Don’t overshare personal details like address, phone number, or passwords.  
- Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.  
- Don’t copy or plagiarize content from others without proper credit.  
- Don’t ignore your privacy and security settings; always review them.
