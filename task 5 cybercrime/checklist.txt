Cyber Crime Reflection

While researching cyberbullying, I was surprised by how widespread and persistent it can be. Even a single post or message can be shared and viewed by hundreds of people, creating long-lasting emotional stress for the victim. I did not realize that cyberbullying could affect mental health so severely, sometimes leading to anxiety, depression, or social isolation. One habit I will personally change is to be more careful with what I post online and to review my privacy settings regularly. I will also report inappropriate content immediately and support friends or peers who might be victims of cyberbullying. Learning about these risks has made me more aware of the importance of responsible online behavior.
